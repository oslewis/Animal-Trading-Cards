Arctic Fox

The arctic fox is an incredibly hardy animal that can survive frigid Arctic temperatures as low as -58Â°F.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes Iagopus.
  • Diet: Omnivore.
  • Average Life Span: 3 to 6 years.
  • Habitat: Arctic Circle.

Arctic foxes have beautiful white (or blue-gray) coats that act as very effective winter camouflage. The natural hues allow the animal to blend into the tundra's ubiquitous snow and ice.
